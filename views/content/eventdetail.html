ABOUT Ma in Transit

The play deals with how a son (played by Makarand) tries to pro-long the delay of his mother's last rites, so that he can spend more time with her (played by Late Amjad Khan's Daughter Ahlam Khan). A deeply moving play taking back the human mind to realizations and forbidden conversations.

Written & Directed by:

Makarand Desphande
